How to create a Blockchain poll?
  • Create your poll with defining its name, its unuique business id and any number of questions and options for the questions.
  • Once you poll is saved, you can register users entering their email address or sending the registration link to the user you want to vote on the poll.
  • The core information of a poll is immutable, once it is saved it can not be modifed, only a new one can be created.
