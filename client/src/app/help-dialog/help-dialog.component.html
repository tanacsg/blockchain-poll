How to create a Blockchain poll?
  • Create your poll with defining its name, its unique business id (poll id) and some questions with selectable options.
  • The poll id can be used in the future to look up the poll, please do not forget to keep a note of the poll id.
  • Once your poll is saved, you can register users entering their email addresses and they receive their ballot code via email.
  • Alternatively, you can share the registration link with users who you want to involve in the poll. The users can then register themself with their email addresses.
  • The core information of a poll is immutable (id, questions, name etc.), once it is saved it can not be modified, only a new one can be created.
